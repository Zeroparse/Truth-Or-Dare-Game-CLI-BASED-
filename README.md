# Truth-Or-Dare-Game-CLI-BASED-
A simplistic truth or Dare game for two or more players.
Here are the rules: 
1. Once your Truth or Dare is assigned, you cannot switch options. You are locked in
2. No bail outs!
3. Truths must be specific and complete. Vague or "I don't remember" answers are a fail; the group can force a Dare instead 
4. All Dares must be completed within five minutes of being assigned, or you fail 
5. The host can immediately cancel any Dare that is dangerous, illegal, or destructive, and issue a replacement 
6. Enter Q at anytime to end the game
